About

Joanne enjoys gaining diverse experiences and exploring passions ranging from coding to music.

Holds a master in Environmental Science, she loves working in scientific modelling and analytics space. She was part of the Robogals' committee board of directors, a non-for-profit organisation that promotes science and engineering to young girls. In her free time, you can find her playing games, fangirling over movies and video game soundtracks.

Music inspires Joanne in a way that coding does.

Joanne is also a harpist, pianist and arranger who strives to convey her music interpretations through her harp, piano and audio production skills. Constantly inspired by video game and film music, she likes experimenting with soundscape, synths and ambience. Since 2015, Joanne has worked in various projects via remote collaboration, drawing on her arranging, performance and music technology work experiences.
