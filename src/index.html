Hello! Welcome to Joanne's space.

Joanne is from Malaysia. She loves food (who doesnt?) and take pictures of food, nature and sometimes kookaburra. When she's not analysing data, she plays and writes for harp and piano. Constantly inspired by orchestra influences, she likes to explore and produce music with soundscape, synths and ambience.

In her free time, you can find her playing games, fangirling over movies and video game soundtracks.
